# Product

## Overview

**What:** Dental staffing platform connecting hygienists with offices for flexible shift work
**For:** Dental hygienists seeking flexible work, dental offices needing temporary/permanent staff

## User Roles

| Role | Access | Key Screens |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Dental Hygienist | Browse shifts, view earnings, find next shift | Landing page, App (mocked) |
| Dental Office | Post shifts, find hygienists | Landing page |

## Key Flows

- Hygienist browses available shifts and applies
- Office posts open shifts and fills them with qualified hygienists
- Both roles sign up via landing page CTAs

## Terminology

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Shift | A temporary work assignment at a dental office |
| Instant pay | Immediate payment availability after completing a shift |
